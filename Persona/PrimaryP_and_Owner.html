Scenarios

Also, she uses the app to locate certain ingredient based on a few details such as price, geographic availability, risk factors, quality or composition.

    • Aims to minimize food waste and associated costs by optimizing inventory management and reducing spoilage.

In order to provide a good customer experience, it is necessary to constantly have the necessary food to prepare the desired dishes. When certain foods are missing, this becomes difficult. On the other hand, too much food leads to wastage if it is purchased more than necessary.
Detailed Description

Iulian is a 48-year-old restaurant owner. His practical goals revolve around efficient restaurant operations and reducing food waste to maximize profits. He wants to implement a smart fridge solution that helps streamline workflows, optimizes inventory management and ensures the freshness of stored food items.

From a business perspective, Iulian's goals include cost reduction through minimizing food waste and associated costs. By optimizing inventory management, he aims to reduce spoilage and avoid unnecessary expenses. Additionally, he recognizes that delivering fresh and high-quality food is essential for customer satisfaction and aims to enhance the overall dining experience.

Iulian emphasizes the need for seamless integration with existing restaurant management systems and processes. Finally, he expects the smart fridge to have a user-friendly interface, enabling non-technical staff members, including the chef, to easily use and navigate the system.

Even if he's not the one using the app, it's useful in that his chefs are able to get the best products, which is a plus for his restaurant.

Scenarios

Scenario: Various ingredient suggestions

In the restaurant kitchen the staff is always busy. When food is needed for certain dishes, it is desired that it be of a high quality, at the best price and available in a nearby area. The Best Goods Recommender app suggests the best ingredients based on price, quality and availability. These ingredients are incorporated into new dishes, giving customers unique and exciting culinary experiences.
