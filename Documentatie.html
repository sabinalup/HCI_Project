Introduction

1.1 Introduction

BEGR is an application that recommends the products, while considering restrictions such as quality, price, for a set of desired goodies, including several information of interest such as characteristics, price, geographic availability. The application also can see whether products/services on the basis of the user's social graph (his/her "friends" on a social network). This application uses QR code scanning to enable users to connect their smart fridges to their phone. Geographic availability is also taken into account, as the app displays nearby stores on a map view, highlighting the availability and pricing of desired goods.

1.3 Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions

The app is designed for a wide range of users, including individuals who may be health-conscious or seek a way to make their shopping experiences easier and cheaper. The app also caters to users who are open to exploring new products and services based on recommendations from their social network friends.

1.4 Purpose

The purpose of BEGR is to provide users with an easy to use tool when is comes to making purchasing decisions. The app aims to simplify the shopping process through factors such as product price, restrictions, and geographic availability. It helps users find the best products that align with their preferences and social connections.

2.2 User Classes and Characteristics

We anticipate that the app will be used by various people who are in charge of managing the food available on location, necessary for preparing dishes. It can be used in both private homes and restaurants by those who are responsible of the cooking meals, regardless of level of education or experience, as it is easy to use due to its simple design. Moreover, it is intended for chefs who want to prepare food with the highest quality ingredients at the most convenient price.

2.3 Operating Environment

The app can be used both on a phone and tablet with an internet connection and in a smart fridge implementation.

2.4 Design and Implementation Constraints

Most of the constraints are directed towards the smart fridge application. In order to complete and make purchases, it is necessary to be connected to the app on the phone or tablet, where the corresponding user information is stored. This action is done through the QR code.
